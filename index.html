Free Workout Tips

Consistency is key! Warm up before workouts, focus on compound exercises, balance strength and cardio. Track your progress, eat clean, and rest properly.

Bulk Plan (Free)

Focus on progressive overload with heavy compound lifts. Eat a calorie surplus with high protein meals. Example weekly plan:
- Monday: Chest & Triceps
- Tuesday: Back & Biceps
- Wednesday: Rest / Active recovery
- Thursday: Legs
- Friday: Shoulders & Arms
- Saturday: Full body / Cardio
- Sunday: Rest

Cutting Plan (Free)

Focus on calorie deficit with high protein, moderate carbs, and low fat. Include HIIT cardio and resistance training. Example weekly plan:
- Monday: Full Body Strength + Cardio
- Tuesday: HIIT + Core
- Wednesday: Rest / Mobility
- Thursday: Upper Body + Cardio
- Friday: Lower Body + Core
- Saturday: Active Recovery
- Sunday: Rest
